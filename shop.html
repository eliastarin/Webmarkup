Albert Heijn

It is the largest supermarket chain in The Netherlands and offers everything from fresh groceries to traditional Dutch cheese (which should be tried for sure by anyone who visits this country) or vegan food. It is for sure the best choice if you want quality products, but the only downside is that it is a bit expensive, thus not making it the perfect choice for a student with a limited budget.

Program: 08:00 - 22:00

Location: Zandvoortselaan 169
