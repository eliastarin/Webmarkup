Bikes 4 All

Probably the most important thing a person should consider before moving to The Netherlands is getting a bike to move around. Being a small country, it is very easy to get from point A to point B using only a bike and walking. For someone who is in a hurry and can’t find anything to buy from the internet, this place is the perfect choice, offering very good running bikes at an affordable price for rent.
