Heemstede Green Spaces

Heemstede has a lot of urban green spaces, the most well-known being the biggest park in town, Buitenplaats Leyduin, which is the best choice for those who want to spend some time in nature and breathe non-polluted air. It is also very good for people with animals, as the park has many routes where one can walk with a dog or bike through the forest.

Rich families once only used Buitenplaats Leyduin as a summer residence. Now everyone can enjoy the old forests, beautiful avenues, birds, exuberant bracken, and deer here all year round. Walking, cycling, or an excursion, it's all possible here!
